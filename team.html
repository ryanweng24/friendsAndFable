MESSAGE FROM THE FOUNDER

Friends & Fable started because of my personal desire to do one simple thing: to connect the dance community that I have grown to love.

It was in San Diego where I first got introduced to the dance community as I know it today. While it was a new and scary experience at first, the welcoming and friendly culture helped me feel safe to step outside of my comfort zone and learn to express myself. I grew to love the movement and soon dancing became a very intimate subject for me. For me, dancing wasn't just about the exercise or some past time activity I did for fun; my greatest life lessons, personal growth, and friendships were made because of my involvement within the community. I can now proudly say that being a "dancer" is part of who I am today. In my short time in San Diego, I have had many opportunities to be involved in different dance affairs. From taking part in a few collegiate teams, to learning and teaching choreography; from dancing in projects alongside extremely talented peers, to helping run a full scale hip hop show... the list goes on. This experience thus far has been, for a lack of better words, life changing.

Above all, the one thing that has never failed to blow my mind is the people that I've had the pleasure of meeting along the way. They have insanely inspiring unique ideas and visions stemming from their experience in dancing. From outreach efforts to unite different communities to putting on benefit concerts to motivate people to follow their dreams... these folks are crazy! You'd seriously think that they were superhuman with the amount of heart they put into what they do.

Their adventures and stories they continue to share are the motivations behind Friends & Fable because they inspire me to want to be a part of the creative process. I just kept telling myself that I would love nothing than to be involved in their project and see their goal through them. And that's the magic! I truly believe when the right people meet other right people at the right time, great things are bound to happen.

My purpose in starting this website is not to try and turn a profit, to compete with other dance blogs, or to gain personal publicity. It is my desire to share the projects that really matter and have an underlying goal to see the dance community grow. Thank you for taking the time to read this message, it is my sincerest hope that you will find something here to take away with you so you can also start your own project. If you find that you may need a helping hand or just want to talk, I invite you to reach out to us here at F&F!

Best,
Tracy L.
