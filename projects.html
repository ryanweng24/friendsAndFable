"Let Me Out" Concept Video

Personal message from the choreographer: “When I heard this song, I thought the musicality would work for a contemporary piece. Banks’ “You Should Have Known” had the dynamics and mood for a very emotional dance. However, it was the lyrics of the song that really drew me in. The lyrics touched on my struggle to please my mother. I knew being a dancer would break my mother’s heart but I can only be me if I had my solitude from her. Because I felt that the emotion needed to be more organic, I used improvisation instead of set choreography to convey the emotion.” - Matthew Tay
