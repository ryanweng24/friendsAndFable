OUR FABLE

Friend & Fable is comprised of creative projects made possible through the joint efforts of dance enthusiasts. It is our mission that Friends & Fable will be a place to seek inspiration and enable awesome ideas to come to life. We believe that there is something unique about dancing that has the ability to make a real impact on people's lives...and we're on a constant search for that connection. Our aim is to contribute to the overall growth and health of the dance community through collaborative projects; encouraging our audiences to see our community in a new light.
